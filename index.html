An Introduction...

As software has continued to grow in popularity, user interfaces and design have also grown. In the past, usability was the main focus, but now researchers are seeing that there is a possible relationship between usability and aesthetics, especially in mobile applications. Certain aspects of components on the screen can influence the way a user will use the app, and even give a user an impression on whether they will continue to use the app. In addition, companies have seen the significance of good design, and, like Apple and Google, have outlined good practices for developers. This paper will explore the relationship between aesthetics and the user experience.
